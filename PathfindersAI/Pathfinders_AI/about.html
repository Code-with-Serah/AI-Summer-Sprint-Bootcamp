About Us

We're a team of six students building a chatbot to support fellow students with studying and career planning powered by Gemini API.

Our Mission

To create a smart, supportive tool that helps students learn more, stress less, and plan better when tackling academic challenges.

  • Provide 24/7 academic support
  • Simplify career path decisions
  • Make learning accessible to all
